Who We Are

Are you looking for meaningful change and support in your life? Congratulations on taking this important first step! My name is Erin Noel, and I am a Registered Nurse and Board Certified Nurse Coach. I'm here to guide you toward your health and wellness goals through a holistic, non-medicinal approach.

With over 30 years of experience in healthcare, specifically in cancer care, in addition to working on personal health and wellness at large, I additionally focus on chronic illness and the issues around it. I understand the challenges chronic illness brings – both personally and professionally. I have navigated the complexities of caregiving while balancing parenthood and a demanding career.

My mission is to provide compassionate, personalized support for whatever challenges you face.

Services

1. Health and Lifestyle Management

Our goal is to achieve lasting wellness by personalized guidance in nutrition and dietary support – we offer a review of your current food choices, habits, encourage a systematic self review and maintain a journal.

2. Coaching the Coach

Are you new to coaching and unsure of your next steps? I've been in your shoes. I'll help you build confidence, refine your skills, and establish a successful coaching practice—guiding you from uncertainty to clarity.

3. Oncology Navigation

With over 30 years of oncology experience, if you or a loved one is facing a cancer diagnosis, I'm here to help you navigate the journey—from diagnosis through treatment and into survivorship. I currently hold a ONS® Chemotherapy and Immunotherapy provider card.

Learn more about nurse coaching outcomes in cancer care

4. Compassion and Caregiver Fatigue

Caring for others can be deeply rewarding but also exhausting. Whether you are a healthcare provider, first responder, military personnel, or caring for a loved one, compassion fatigue and burnout are real challenges. I offer individual and group support.
